E-commerce Thrift Store Wireframe

This wireframe provides a high-level layout for a thrift clothing e-commerce website, focusing on a clean, user-friendly experience. The design prioritizes showcasing products, simplifying the shopping process, and building a sense of community around sustainable fashion.

Header

    Top Bar:

        Promotional Banner: "Free shipping on orders over $75!" or "New arrivals daily!"

    Main Navigation Bar:

        Logo: Top-left corner, links to the homepage.

        Search Bar: Centered and prominent, with a placeholder like "Search for a vintage tee, a brand, or a style..."

        Navigation Links:

            Shop: Dropdown menu with categories (Men's, Women's, Accessories, Sale).

            New Arrivals: Highlights the latest additions.

            How It Works: Explains the thrifting process, item condition, and sustainability mission.

            About Us: Story of the brand and its values.

        User Icons (Right Side):

            Wishlist/Favorites: A heart icon.

            Account: A person icon, links to user profile, orders, etc.

            Cart: A shopping bag icon with a number indicating items in the cart.

Homepage

    Hero Section:

        Large, full-width banner with high-quality photos of people wearing the clothes.

        Catchy headline: "Find Your Next Favorite Piece" or "Sustainable Style, Unique Finds."

        Call-to-action (CTA) button: "Shop Now" or "Explore Collections."

    Featured Categories:

        Three to four visual cards with images representing key categories like "Vintage Denim," "Cozy Sweaters," or "Summer Dresses."

        Each card has a clear heading and a "Shop Now" button.

    New Arrivals/Just In:

        Horizontal scrollable section displaying 8-12 new product cards.

        Each product card includes:

            Image of the item.

            Item name.

            Price.

            A small badge or icon indicating its condition (e.g., "Like New," "Vintage," "Gently Used").

            Hover effect shows a quick-add-to-cart or wishlist option.

    Why Thrift with Us? (Value Proposition):

        A section with three to four simple icons and short descriptions highlighting the brand's unique selling points:

            Icon 1: "Eco-Friendly" - A leaf icon.

            Icon 2: "Quality Checked" - A checkmark or tag icon.

            Icon 3: "Affordable Style" - A price tag icon.

            Icon 4: "Unique Finds" - A star or diamond icon.

    Customer Testimonials/Social Proof:

        A carousel of quotes from happy customers.

        Can also include an Instagram feed section showing customers wearing the clothes with a branded hashtag.

    Email Subscription:

        Simple form with an input field for email and a "Subscribe" button.

        Headline: "Stay in the Loop on New Drops."

Product Listing Page (PLP)

    Title/Breadcrumbs:

        Page Title: "Men's T-Shirts" or "All Dresses."

        Breadcrumbs: "Home > Shop > Men's > T-Shirts."

    Left Sidebar (Filters):

        Categories: Collapsible lists (e.g., T-Shirts, Jackets, Pants).

        Size: Checkboxes (XS, S, M, L, XL).

        Condition: Radio buttons or checkboxes (New with Tags, Excellent, Gently Used, Vintage).

        Price: Slider or input fields (Min/Max).

        Brand: Searchable list of brands (Levi's, Nike, etc.).

        Color: Swatches of different colors.

    Main Content Area:

        Sort By: Dropdown menu (e.g., "Newest," "Price: Low to High," "Best Match").

        Product Grid:

            Displays products in a clean grid layout (3-4 items per row).

            Each product card is the same as the one on the homepage.

Product Detail Page (PDP)

    Main Image Gallery:

        Large main image on the left.

        Clickable thumbnails below or to the side for additional angles.

    Product Details (Right Side):

        Product Name: Large, bold font.

        Price: Prominently displayed.

        Condition: Badge/label (e.g., "Vintage," "Gently Used").

        Description:

            Bullet points for key details (Material, Size, Measurements).

            Paragraph text for a more detailed story about the item or its features.

        Add to Cart Section:

            Quantity Selector: Simple +/- buttons.

            Add to Cart Button: Large and a contrasting color.

        Shipping & Returns Information:

            Collapsible sections with details on policies.

    You Might Also Like/Related Items:

        Horizontal scrollable section showing similar products to encourage more browsing.

Cart/Checkout

    Cart Page:

        List of Items: Shows each item with an image, name, price, quantity selector, and a "Remove" button.

        Order Summary: Box on the side showing Subtotal, Shipping Cost (if applicable), and Total.

        CTA Button: "Proceed to Checkout."

    Checkout Process (Multi-step):

        Step 1: Contact Information: Fields for name, email, and phone.

        Step 2: Shipping Address: Fields for street, city, state, zip.

        Step 3: Shipping Method: Options with estimated delivery times and costs.

        Step 4: Payment: Fields for credit card details, PayPal, etc.

        Order Review: Final summary of the order before the user confirms.

Footer

    About Section: Short paragraph about the brand.

    Customer Service Links:

        Contact Us

        FAQ

        Shipping & Returns

        Size Guide

    Social Media Icons: Links to Instagram, Pinterest, Facebook, etc.

    Copyright Info: © [Your Brand Name] [Year].

    Newsletter Signup (Repeated from Homepage).
